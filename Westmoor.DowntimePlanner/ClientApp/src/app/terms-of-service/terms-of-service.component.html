Right to be Forgotten

We respect your right to be forgotten, but just how most of our system still require manual actions when new users joins, we does not currently let you delete your account yourself. For now, please contact a maintainer of the application via GitHub, either through a GitHub issue, email or other means (if available). We will make sure to erase all of you data and said GitHub issue within a few days.

Analytics are stored for up to 90 days and will be purged automatically passed that point. No action can be taken to erase them prematurely.
